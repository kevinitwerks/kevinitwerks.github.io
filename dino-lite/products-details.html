AM4515ZT DINO-LITE EDGE

The 4515 series introduces auto magnification reading (AMR) features to detect and display the magnification rate. No more manual entry or ambiguity of the actual magnification.

  • 200x

    Magnify from 20x to 50x at a distance and achieve an additional 200x when the Dino-Lite is at close range.

  • Auto Magnification Reading (AMR)

    Magnification rate automatically detected in the software. AMR makes taking measurements more efficient, accurate, and easier.

  • Polarizer

    The polarizer enhances the contrast on reflective surfaces by reducing or eliminating light reflections.

  • Scroll Lock

    Use the scroll lock to fix the scroll to assure a fixed position for magnification or working distance.

  • 1.3 MP High Res Imaging

    High-resolution SXGA (1280x1024). It features a state-of-the-art sensor for images with stunning sharpness and minimal aberration and vignetting.

  • USB 2.0 Connection

    Digitize your microscopy experience; allow easy recording and sharing of observations with a compatible PC or MAC.
