Wood Composite

Wood is a natural, feel-good material, creating warmth and comfort, exactly what is needed for the interior of a home.

Aluminium on the other hand, is a robust protective shield against the elements. Put these two materials together and they form an Aluminium-clad wood window which lasts generations without the need for continuous maintenance.

The stunning result of such a combination is only used by the most discerning of people, where differentiation is in their DNA.

Benefits

Brand advantage 1: Durability

UNILUX has always used the highest-quality materials. Down to the finest detail: instead of the inferior silicon, we use smooth glazing seals made from synthetic rubber. The high-quality workmanship and strict quality controls ensure you gain long-term enjoyment from your UNILUX window.

Brand advantage 2: Energy efficiency

Each of our aluminium clad wood products has state-of-the-art warm-edge spacers between the glass panes, ensuring better thermal values, greater comfort near the window, and significantly reduced condensation build-up during colder months.

Brand advantage 3: Safety

UNILUX locking parts are made from high-quality materials. Mushroom pins are also a standard feature of all our aluminium clad wood window fittings.

Product - Windows

Experience the Difference

Sapphire Windows uses world-class systems developed by Deceuninck. Our windows have been designed with emphasis on security and simplicity. Innovative hardware technology ensures that you are well protected against all elements, both natural and human.

The surrounding environment provides light, warmth and fresh air, and our windows are able to let these desirable elements in when required, yet remain secure and well insulated both thermally and acoustically when closed. The multi-chambered profiles of the window are the reason such insulations are realised, while the galvanished steel reinforcements located in the heart of the profiles ensure strength and security of the windows.

Glazing options are available from 6mm to 31mm, with the type of glass an option to you. Choose either single glazed float glass, double glazed, tempered or laminated glass to suit your needs.

Absolute Top in Insulation

Engineering tests carried out in Germany have shown that our windows are able reduce sound transmittance (soundproofing) and achieve a maximum Sound Transmission Class (STC) of 38dB. A maximum heat transfer coefficient U=1.6w/m2K is also possible, which is up to 4 times better than traditional aluminium windows. View our real world acoustic test at the bottom of this page!

Product - DOORS

Are You on the Safe Side?

Sapphire Windows uses world class systems developed by Deceuninck. Our doors have been designed and developed with security as priority.

Multi-point locking mechanisms are employed in all our doors, which greatly increase security. Our doors are multi-chambered, which mean that they have both sound and thermal insulating properties, just like our windows. The multitude of designs we have for doors ensure that there is always a design to suit your needs for your home.

Glazing options are available from 6mm to 31mm, with the type of glass also as an option. Choose from either single-glazed float glass, double glazed, tempered or laminated glass to suit your needs.

Unsurpassed Insulation

Engineering tests carried out in Germany have shown that our doors are able to achieve a maximum STC of 40dB (40dB reduction in sound) and a maximum heat transfer coefficient U=1.6w/m2K.
