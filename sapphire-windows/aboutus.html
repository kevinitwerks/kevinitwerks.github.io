ABOUT SAPPHIRE WINDOWS

Established in 1989, Sapphire Windows is a subsidiary of Sapphire-JLE Pte Ltd (SJLE) and is the sole agent and fabricator of Deceuninck uPVC (unplasticised Polyvinyl Chloride) Window and Door Systems in Singapore. The office, showroom and factory are located on a 27,000 square feet premise at 2 Loyang Drive.

Our policy is to continually provide quality products and services while meeting applicable statutory and regulatory requirements. With this commitment to quality, the company continuously upgrades its management system, products, and machinery to ensure that we are always on par with industry standards.

In 2001, Sapphire Windows was the first uPVC company achieved accreditation for its ISO 9001:2008 management system from the TUV SUD/PSB. It is also an approved window fabricator by the Building & Construction Authority (BCA). Certified BizSafe L4 by the Safety and Health council, the product also conforms to PSB product conformity standard, ASTM 1 635-06 and UKAS Quality Management system.

All employees involved in the production and installation of the uPVC system undergo a structured training program either in house or in Europe by Deceuninck’s trainers.

To date, we have completed more than 2,000 projects in Singapore and several overseas projects in Brunei, Indonesia, Myanmar, Philippines, Maldives, Seychelles and Taiwan.

At Sapphire Windows, we pride ourselves in the provision of a world class quality product coupled with the desire to meet and exceed all customer expectations each and every time.

OUR PRINCIPAL

Founded in 1937, Deceuninck is a Belgiancompany premised at its headquarters in Hooglede-Gits. From humble beginnings, it has grown over the years to become one of the leading designers and manufacturers of high quality systems for building sustainable homes for today and the future.

Deceuninck’s commitment to meeting today’s demand for sustainable homes with timeless and elegant design is evident in their products, as they continually improve on their technologies of uPVC extrusion.

With constant research and development, Deceuninck creates innovative building solutions for windows and doors, outdoor living, interior boards, roofline and cladding. With a service life of 50 years, Deceuninck allows for the option to recycle and reuse their products once that perios ends.

Deceuninck is one of the top 3 industry players in Europe in its sector and is active in more than 75 countries across Europe, North America and Asia. The company is home to 2,821 employees, of which 670 are in Belgium working in one of its 35 production and/or sales facilities. Deceuninck has realised a worldwide sales amounting to more than 536 million Euros and is a listed companyon the Euronext stock exchange.

OUR GUARANTEE

At Sapphire Windows, it is our first and utmost priority to ensure that our products are free from defects and irregularities, and will repair or replace any defective profiles/hardware at no cost to the owner*.

Our profile warranty guards against potential defects such as:
  • warping,
  • discolouration,
  • cracking, and
  • rotting.

We provide a 10-year warranty for White Profiles and a 7-year warranty for Coloured Profiles.
